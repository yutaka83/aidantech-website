About Us

Our Company

Aidan Technologies is a world-class digital agency based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, that knows about the challenges the digital revolution poses for your business or organization. We work together with you to turn these challenges into opportunities and develop cutting-edge digital solutions that are tailored to you.

We began as a small digital firm, and that passion for design continues to define us today. We believe the best interactive experiences come from the sweet harmony of smart design thinking and innovative use of technology.

Aidan Technologies is a subsidiary of Aidan Group of Companies.

Since 2007

Timeline

We began as a small digital firm, but have since grown leaps and bounds to be where we are today.

2007 · Oct

How We Started

Aidan Technologies was officially registered with the Companies Commission of Malaysia (791404-P) as an Information Technology company, specializing in web and system development — via the incubator program at the Centre for Commercialization and Technopreneur Development (CCTD), Multimedia University (MMU), Cyberjaya.

2008 · Jan

First Project

Rented a small office room in Petaling Jaya, Selangor. Developed an operation system for a prominent pest control company, and the registration system for Persatuan Sains Matematik Malaysia (PERSAMA). Tan Sri Ani Arope was appointed as the Chairman.

2009 · Jan

First System

Moved to Taman Tun Dr Ismail, Kuala Lumpur. Developed our first product, Sistem Pengurusan Aset dan Tugas (SPATU), plus a Job Recruitment System and Commission Calculation System. Registered with e-Perolehan.

2010 · Feb

e-Commerce

Developed Lot.my, an award-winning e-commerce platform.

2011 · Jan

MGPWA Portal

Moved to a new office in Setapak, Kuala Lumpur. Delivered a project with Hospital Ampang — our first project under the MAMPU MGPWA guideline. Won the NEF-Awani Award for New Media Application and were shortlisted as a finalist in the .my innovation Award for Most Innovative Website.

2012 · Aug

First Open House

Received the Best New Media Application award and expanded the office next door. Held the first Aidan Open House, attended by close to 300 people.

2013 · Jan

New Chairman

Dato Nik Mohd Amin was appointed as the new chairman following the passing of Tan Sri Ani Arope. Won our first tender project and received 5-star ratings.

2014 · Mar

Developed mySistem

Developed new products: MyAduan, MyTempahan, MyLogin, and MySSO.

2015 · Apr

Mobile Apps Division

Launched MyTender, secured key clients such as SPRM, and started venturing into the Mobile App Development division.

2016 · Jul

Kampung Pengantin

Held the 4th Open House at a hall as the company continued to grow its team and client base.

Partner With Us

Referral Program

Basically, you send us business, and we will give you money. That's it.

1

Discover a potential customer that is seriously searching for a business solution relevant to our expertise.

2

Pass their contact details to us, including the type of solution they are looking for. We'll do the homework.

3

We call the potential customer for a first meeting. It's best if you can join us — that makes it a real powerhouse meeting.

4

We take care of the remaining steps. If we successfully close the deal, just wait for your commission payment.

Terms & Conditions

  • Commission is 10% from sales. Web hosting, domain registration and off-the-shelf items are excluded — valid only for custom software, portal and mobile app development.
  • For private companies, commission is effective upon at least 40% deposit payment. For government agencies, it's effective upon 100% payment (no progress plan) or a minimum 20% initial payment (structured progress plan).
  • If you refer a deal we already know, there is no commission.
  • Commission applies to the 1st project only — no commission for succession projects with the same customer unless they contact us through you again.
  • No recurring commission structure unless stated otherwise, and no multi-level pay-out — this is not an MLM program.
  • This deal works smoothly only if we have mutual trust between us.
